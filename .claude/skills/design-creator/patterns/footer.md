# Pattern: Footer

This file gives Claude what to assemble, not what to copy.

## 1. Purpose
Close the page: secondary navigation, legal, contact — and a final brand impression.

## 2. Block composition
**Required:** navigation link groups, legal line (copyright), logo / wordmark.
**Optional:** a newsletter signup, social links, a contact block, language/theme switches, a large brand wordmark as a closing statement.

## 3. Order logic
Link groups and brand block in the main band → a thin bottom strip with legal and minor links. Group links by meaning (product / company / resources / legal). The footer is allowed one final brand flourish — a large wordmark, a closing line.

## 4. Variants by mode
- **Clean** — tidy multi-column link layout, restrained.
- **Statement** — an oversized brand wordmark, a kinetic marquee, bold type as a closing statement.

## 5. Technique bindings
- A newsletter field follows `principles/forms.md` (label, inline validation, concrete errors).
- Link columns collapse/stack on mobile (`principles/responsive.md`).
- An oversized wordmark / marquee ties to `principles/kinetic-type.md`.

## 6. Typical mistakes
- An ungrouped wall of links.
- A newsletter input with placeholder-as-label and a vague error.
- Columns that do not stack on mobile.

## 7. The hook in this section
Carry the site's hook through this section too, not just the hero (`principles/concept.md`): state per build how it advances the one central idea, or how it stays deliberately quiet so the hook lands elsewhere.
